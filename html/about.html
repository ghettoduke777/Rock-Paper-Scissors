About Game Day

Welcome to Game Day!

Game Day is a simple, fun, and interactive Rock–Paper–Scissors experience built for players of all ages. Whether you're here to kill a few minutes, challenge your reflexes, or just enjoy a classic game with a modern twist, this project brings the timeless battle of wits right to your screen.

Why This Project Exists

This game was created as part of a personal development journey in web programming. It showcases core front‑end skills such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while keeping the gameplay fast, responsive, and enjoyable.

How It Works

  • You choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  • The computer makes a random choice.
  • The winner is determined instantly based on classic rules.
  • Your score updates as you play.

Future Improvements

Game Day is always evolving. Planned updates include animations, sound effects, difficulty modes, and a leaderboard system.

Thanks for stopping by — and enjoy the game!
